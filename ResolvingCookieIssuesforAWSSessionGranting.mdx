---
title: "Resolving Cookie Issues for AWS Session Granting"
---

If requests to “Initiate AWS Session” are failing in Serval, you may need to allow third-party cookies in your browser for Serval. Follow the correct instructions based on your browser:

<Accordion title="Chrome" icon="sparkles">
  1. Go to **Settings** \> **Privacy and security** \> **Third Party Cookies**.
  2. Under **Sites allowed to use third-party cookies:**

  - Add https://app.serval.com.
  - Add your bridge service URL (e.g. https://serval.security-opal.cf.verkada.com).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Firefox" icon="sparkles">
  1. Go to **Settings** \> **Privacy & Security.**
  2. Select **Manage Exceptions.**
  3. Enter https://app.serval.com, then **Add Exception**.
  4. Enter your bridge server URL (e.g. https://serval.security-opal.cf.verkada.com), then **Add Exception**.
  5. Click **Save Changes.**
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Safari" icon="sparkles">
  1. Go to **Safari** \> **Settings** \> **Privacy**.
  2. Deselect **Block all cookies**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Brave" icon="sparkles">
  1. Go to `brave://settings/cookies`.
  2. Under **Sites that can always use cookies:**

  - Add https://app.serval.com.
  - Add your bridge server URL (e.g. https://serval.security-opal.cf.verkada.com).
</Accordion>
