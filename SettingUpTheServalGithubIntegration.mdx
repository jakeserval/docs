To check your installation, navigate to:

[Installation Page](https://github.com/apps/serval-dev/installations/select_target)

If you see “Cancel Request ❌“ on your desired repo, contact your Github Admin to and ask them to approve the installation request.

![Screenshot2025 04 10at3 09 06PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-10at3.09.06PM.png)

Once its installed successfully, it should show up in the Settings page in the Github UI:

`https://github.com/YOUR_ORG/YOUR_REPO/settings/installations`

Once it is successfully installed, you should “Configure” next to the app:

![Screenshot2025 04 10at3 13 48PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-10at3.13.48PM.png)
